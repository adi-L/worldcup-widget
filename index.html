⚽ Embeddable World Cup Widget

One component, many ways to embed it — each example below shows the exact code. Repo: github.com/adi-L/worldcup-widget

1 · Load it once

Add this <script> to your page once (loads from a CDN — no install, no build):

<!-- once, anywhere in your page -->
<script type="module"
        src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/adi-L/worldcup-widget@main/worldcup-widget.js"></script>

Full card (default)

Drop the tag anywhere you want the scoreboard to appear.

<worldcup-widget></worldcup-widget>

Languages & RTL

en · fr · he (right-to-left) · ar (right-to-left)

<worldcup-widget lang="en"></worldcup-widget>
<worldcup-widget lang="fr"></worldcup-widget>
<worldcup-widget lang="he"></worldcup-widget>  <!-- RTL -->
<worldcup-widget lang="ar"></worldcup-widget>  <!-- RTL -->

Mini — click to expand

A small pill (live score or countdown) that opens the full card on click.

<worldcup-widget variant="mini" lang="he"></worldcup-widget>

Floating launcher (bottom-right ↘)

Pinned to a page corner like a chat bubble — see it live in the bottom-right of this page. Click it to open. 

<worldcup-widget
  variant="mini"
  position="fixed"
  corner="bottom-right"></worldcup-widget>
